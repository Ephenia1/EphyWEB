Our History

Founded in 2018 in Durban, South Africa, Brew & Bean Coffee Co. began as a small artisanal coffee shop focused on quality, sustainability, and community.

Mission

To connect people through exceptional coffee experiences while promoting sustainable sourcing.

Vision

To become the leading artisanal coffee brand in KwaZulu-Natal with a strong community presence and a thriving online store.
